About ARAX

Arax People Services India Pvt Ltd is a leading provider of private security solutions, headquartered in Madhapur, Hyderabad. Founded with the mission to protect lives and assets with utmost professionalism and integrity, we offer a comprehensive range of security services tailored to meet the unique needs of residential communities, corporate offices, commercial establishments, construction sites, and industrial operations.

Established - 2025: We understand our customers and their businesses well and we are committed to perform. We help assess the risks they face now and, in the future, so that we provide top class tailored solutions that stop financial, human, and brand losses.

Key Strengths

  • Innovative Solutions: We specialize in providing cutting-edge services designed to meet the unique needs of each client.
  • Client-centric Approach: Our deep understanding of client businesses enables us to offer world-class solutions that minimize financial, human, and brand-related risks.
